Find out about events related to Tokyo 2020.

While the Olympic and Paralympic Games are a festival of sport, they are also a cultural festival. The Tokyo 2020 Organising Committee is planning to hold the Tokyo 2020 NIPPON Festival as the culmination of its Tokyo 2020 Participation Programme.



Let’s 55 is a project where people can see and experience ALL 55 competitions of the Tokyo 2020 Olympic and Paralympic Games.



Everyone can join in the Olympic and Paralympic Games celebrations at Live Sites located across Japan. At Live Sites, people can watch live coverage of sports competitions on giant screens during Games-time.



TOKYO WATERFRONT CITY will bring competition facilities to an urban setting, providing a new way for fans to experience the Tokyo 2020 Games.



"Make The Beat!”

"Make The Beat!” is a cheering project to let everyone join the Tokyo 2020 Games through social media. Your video might be shown on the big screen at venues.
